About Lendi Alumni Portal

The Lendi Alumni Portal is a dedicated platform created to connect our esteemed alumni with current students, faculty, and each other. This initiative aims to build a strong community where knowledge, opportunities, and memories can be shared.

Our alumni network is spread across the globe, excelling in diverse fields like technology, entrepreneurship, research, and leadership. Through this portal, we encourage collaboration, mentorship, and active participation in the development of our institution.

Key highlights of the Alumni Portal include:

  • Maintaining strong alumni connections
  • Providing networking and mentorship opportunities
  • Sharing news, updates, and success stories
  • Organizing reunions and alumni events
  • Contributing to institutional growth
